A speculative design project about a new mind-altering drug in a hypothetical future. Via the effects of this substance and the assistance of machines and fungi, human users’ consciousness dissolves together to form a mycelium-like, distributed network. Through this system, humans act as individual hypha—meat-based AIs—to lend their computational power to nature in a symbiotic relationship. As such, the speculative concepts surrounding this drug offer us narratives to provide thoughtful critiques about our current conditions.
